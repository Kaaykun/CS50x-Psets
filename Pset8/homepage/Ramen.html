Sapporo Style Miso Ramen

The rich miso based broth is usually topped with sweetcorn, butter, bean sprouts, finely chopped pork and garlic.

Source: Sakuraco Blog, by Jenna Wilson
Tokyo Style Shio Ramen

This dish consists of slightly thin, curly noodles served in shio (salt) flavoured chicken broth topped with scallions, menma, sliced pork, kamaboko, egg, sea weed and spinach.

Source: Zenpop Blog, by Tagu
Hakata Style Tonkotsu Ramen

It has a rich, milky, tonkotsu (pork-bone) broth with thin, straigth noodles. Distinctive toppings such as crushed garlic, pickled ginger, sesame seeds and spicy pickled mustard greens can often be found on the table to modify the dish to ones wish.
